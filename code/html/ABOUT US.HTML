Our Story

We're a team of 12 people undergraduate in the cybersecurity department. We found many dangers from technology, so we decided to do something make people awareness of the dangers of technology. In today's digital age, where technology permeates nearly every aspect of our lives, it's essential for users to be acutely aware of the inherent dangers of cybersecurity threats. From identity theft to financial fraud, the risks posed by malicious actors online are ever-present and evolving.

Users must understand that every click, download, or interaction with technology carries potential risks, and staying vigilant is paramount. Whether it's protecting personal information on social media platforms, securing sensitive data on online accounts, or being wary of suspicious emails, users must be proactive in safeguarding themselves against cyber threats. Furthermore, the proliferation of smart devices and the Internet of Things (IoT) introduces new vulnerabilities, highlighting the need for heightened awareness and diligence.

By educating users about the dangers of cybersecurity threats and technology, we empower them to make informed decisions and take proactive measures to protect themselves and their digital assets. Together, we can build a safer and more secure online environment for everyone.
